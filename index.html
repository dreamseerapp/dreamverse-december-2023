Description:

As the year draws to a close, the Dreamverse continues to evolve, reflecting the myriad emotions and experiences of the Seers. December 2023's collective dream fabric is a poignant representation of the global zeitgeist, colored by threads of angst and hope.

The world today stands at a precipice, balancing between ongoing global conflicts and the persistent echoes of environmental and health crises. This complex backdrop has inevitably seeped into the Dreamverse, with prevalent themes of Anxiety and Fear manifesting vividly in our shared dreamscapes.

These primal emotions, however, are not without a counterbalance. There emerges a consistent thread of Protection intertwined with both of these themes. This motif reflects a defensive stance against the tumultuous forces, and a deep intrinsic yearning for safety.
